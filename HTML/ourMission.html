Our Mission

We all know the biggest challenge in a student’s life: distraction. After reflecting on this, we realized the root cause lies in our increasingly short attention spans, shaped by endless scrolling through social media and consuming short, diverse content.

Instead of battling this desire to watch short videos, we thought, why not use it to help us study?

Introducing LEER, a platform designed to turn procrastination into productivity. LEER offers engaging, bite-sized videos that not only capture your attention but also help you study effectively and stay focused—all without the boredom.

Let’s replace mindless scrolling with meaningful learning. Welcome to LEER.
